About Sun.Star Cebu

Sun.Star Cebu is the top newspaper in Cebu. This Github page hosts some of its digital projects and experiments. If you want to help us and to contribute to our projects, feel free to send a pull request.

Authors and Contributors

Sun.Star Cebu is led by editor-in-chief Isolde Amante (@isoldeamante). This repository is maintained with the help of business editor and tech columnist Max Limpag (@maxlimpag).
